v1.3 Rules Added After CBS #38 Test

These are now baked into generated prompts. They force specificity, CTR architecture, local voice and commercial category visibility.

Local Proof + CTR Engine: every anchor and medium article must include local proof, reader action and a future commercial path.

Placeholder Asset Rule: unsold categories still need useful guides, checklists, polls, maps, forms or nominations.

Category Visibility Rule: every issue should surface 8–12 business category opportunities as reader problems.

Local Voice Without Fake Quotes: include what locals are likely asking or experiencing without inventing named quotes.

County Texture Rule: county-wide editions must use real towns, roads, high streets, journeys, prices and local scenarios, not place-name scattering.
