📝 Content Input Sheet

🔥 Angle Engine — Required
This stops the issue becoming generic. Every issue needs tension, visible reality and reader involvement.
🧠 Issue Memory & Continuity
Use this to stop repetition and follow through on promises from the previous issue.
👔 Expert Partner Inputs
Give each expert a direct warning, useful tip, and quotable line. Real partner names are optional.
⚡ Activation Partner Inputs
Activations should be the main example inside the story, not a small mention. Add honest angle, downside and reader value.
📊 Local Data & Market Stats
Real numbers make the newsletter credible. Paste relevant local data, useful facts or reality checks.
🏘️ Street / Area / Spotlight of the Week
A specific place, street, venue, area or feature to highlight.
📰 Country News & Regulatory Updates
National or country-specific rules, costs, regulations or timely updates.
🎪 Community & Events
Events, community happenings, local dates, seasonal activities or reader-submission prompts.
